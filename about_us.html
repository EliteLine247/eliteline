About Us

Welcome to Damascus Bakery, Brighton’s first Syrian bakery, where authentic flavors and traditional recipes meet modern culinary delights. Located on Western Road, we are dedicated to bringing the rich culinary heritage of Syria to the heart of Brighton.

Our Story

Founded by Mohammad, who journeyed from Syria to the UK as a refugee, Damascus Bakery is a testament to resilience and passion. Mohammad's dream was to create a place where the people of Brighton could experience the true taste of Syrian cuisine. With the support of friends and family, he opened Damascus Bakery, transforming his vision into a beloved community spot.

Our Offerings

At Damascus Bakery, we specialise in a variety of Syrian savory pastries such as Chicken Shawarma, Muhammara, Za’atar, and Safihah, all freshly baked in-house. Our menu has expanded to include full Syrian breakfasts, delectable desserts like baklava, and traditional Middle Eastern dishes. We also offer a cozy seating area where customers can enjoy their meals and socialise.

Community and Quality

We pride ourselves on our great service and friendly atmosphere. Our bakery has quickly become a favorite among locals and visitors alike, thanks to our delicious food and reasonable prices. We use only the freshest ingredients, ensuring that every bite is packed with authentic flavors that transport you to the streets of Damascus.

Catering Services

In addition to our in-house offerings, we provide catering for various events including weddings, work conferences, and community gatherings. Our catering menu can be customised to include a wide range of Syrian dishes, tailored to meet the specific needs of your event.

Join Us

Whether you are craving a quick falafel wrap, a hearty Syrian breakfast, or looking to cater an event, Damascus Bakery is here to serve you. Come visit us and experience the warmth of Syrian hospitality and the richness of its culinary traditions.
